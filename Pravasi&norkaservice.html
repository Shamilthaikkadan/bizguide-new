NORKA Services

"We provide NORKA services, assisting NRIs with employment, document attestation, financial aid, legal support, healthcare services, and reintegration for returning Keralites.

  • Employment Support: Facilitating job opportunities, skill development programs, and vocational training for Keralites seeking employment abroad.
  • Attestation Services: Providing attestation and authentication of documents such as educational certificates and employment contracts for use abroad.
  • Financial Assistance: Offering financial aid, loans, and scholarships to eligible NRIs for various purposes, including education, healthcare, and entrepreneurship.
  • Legal Aid: Legal consultation, assistance with disputes, and support for NRIs facing legal challenges abroad.
  • Return and Reintegration: Assistance with repatriation, resettlement, and reintegration services for Keralites returning to Kerala from abroad.
  • Healthcare Services: Providing access to healthcare facilities, medical insurance, and assistance for medical emergencies for NRIs.
  • Education Support: Guidance on educational opportunities, admissions, scholarships, and academic counseling for NRIs and their families.
  • Investment Opportunities: Promoting investment opportunities and entrepreneurship initiatives for NRIs interested in investing in Kerala.
