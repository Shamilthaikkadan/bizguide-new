Privacy policy

1. Collection of Personal Information: Specify what types of personal information you collect from visitors, such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, and addresses.

2. Purpose of Information Collection: Clearly outline the purposes for which you collect personal information, such as processing bookings, providing customer support, and improving services.

3. Use of Cookies: Explain your use of cookies and similar tracking technologies, including the types of cookies used, their purposes, and how visitors can manage their cookie preferences.

4. Data Security Measures: Describe the security measures you have in place to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

5. Data Retention Policy: Specify how long you retain personal information and the criteria used to determine retention periods.

6. Third-Party Disclosure: Disclose whether you share personal information with third parties, such as service providers or business partners, and for what purposes.

7. User Rights: Inform users of their rights regarding their personal information, including the right to access, correct, update, or delete their data.

8. Legal Basis for Processing: Explain the legal basis for processing personal information, such as consent, contract fulfillment, or legitimate interests.

9. International Data Transfers: If applicable, disclose whether personal information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside of Saudi Arabia and explain the safeguards in place to ensure the security of such transfers.

10. Policy Updates: Reserve the right to update your privacy policy periodically and provide information on how users will be notified of any changes.
