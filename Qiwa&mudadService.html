Mudad Services

We provide streamlined vehicle registration, licensing, and traffic enforcement services through Mudad, ensuring safety and efficiency on Saudi roads.

  • Vehicle Registration: Facilitating the registration of new vehicles and renewal of existing registrations.
  • Driver's Licensing: Assisting individuals in obtaining or renewing driver's licenses.
  • Traffic Violation Management: Managing traffic violations, issuing fines, and implementing traffic regulations to ensure road safety.
  • Vehicle Inspection: Conducting vehicle inspections to ensure compliance with safety and emissions standards.
  • Road Safety Education: Providing educational programs and initiatives to raise awareness about road safety and responsible driving practices.
  • Accident Investigation: Investigating traffic accidents and providing support services to victims and their families.
  • Vehicle Ownership Transfer: Facilitating the transfer of vehicle ownership between individuals.
  • Vehicle Renewal Services: Assisting with the renewal of vehicle registrations and licenses.
  • Online Services: Providing online platforms and tools for vehicle registration, licensing, and traffic violation payment.
  • Customer Support: Offering assistance and guidance to individuals for all Mudad-related services, addressing any inquiries or concerns they may have.
